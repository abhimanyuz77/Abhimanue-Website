24-WEEK CURRICULUM

Complete NEET Physics in 20 Weeks + 4 Weeks of Full Revision & Tests

01

Phase 1: Units, Motion & Laws (Weeks 1–4)

  • Physical World & Units

    Units, dimensions, errors & significant figures — essential numerical precision.

  • Kinematics

    1D & 2D motion, projectile motion, graphs, and relative velocity.

  • Laws of Motion

    Newton’s laws, friction, circular motion, pseudo forces, tension & normal reaction.

  • Concept Drills & PYQs

    Practice on motion & force problems from past NEET papers.

02

Phase 2: Work, Energy & Rotational Dynamics (Weeks 5–8)

  • Work, Power & Energy

    Work-energy theorem, conservation of energy, collisions, potential energy curves.

  • System of Particles & Rotation

    Torque, angular momentum, moment of inertia, rolling motion.

  • Gravitation

    Universal law, potential, satellites, energy of orbits, variation of g.

  • PYQ Practice Set

    NEET-level numericals on energy, rotation, and gravitation.

03

Phase 3: Heat, Thermodynamics & Waves (Weeks 9–12)

  • Thermal Properties of Matter

    Heat transfer, expansion, calorimetry, Newton’s cooling.

  • Thermodynamics

    First law, second law, Carnot engine, efficiency & entropy.

  • Kinetic Theory

    Pressure, mean free path, degrees of freedom, molecular speeds.

  • Waves & Oscillations

    SHM, resonance, beats, Doppler effect, superposition.

04

Phase 4: Electrostatics to AC Circuits (Weeks 13–16)

  • Electrostatics

    Electric field, potential, Gauss’s law, capacitors & energy storage.

  • Current Electricity

    Ohm’s law, resistors, Wheatstone bridge, Kirchhoff’s laws, power dissipation.

  • Magnetic Effects

    Biot–Savart, Ampere’s law, Lorentz force, cyclotron, motion in fields.

  • EM Induction & Alternating Current

    Faraday’s & Lenz’s laws, self/mutual induction, transformers, RMS values.

05

Phase 5: Optics, Modern Physics & Electronics (Weeks 17–20)

  • Ray Optics

    Lenses, mirrors, refraction, reflection, optical instruments.

  • Wave Optics

    Interference, diffraction, polarization, Young’s double slit.

  • Modern Physics

    Photoelectric effect, Bohr model, de Broglie, radioactivity, nuclear physics.

  • Semiconductors & Devices

    PN junctions, diodes, transistors, logic gates, basic circuits.

06

Phase 6: Practice, Tests & Revision (Weeks 21–24)

  • Weekly Topic-wise Tests

    Timed NEET-style tests for every major unit (Mechanics to Optics).

  • Full-Length Mock Tests

    Complete 180-mark Physics sections under real exam conditions.

  • Formula Marathon & Concept Recap

    Rapid revision sessions covering all key equations and error-prone topics.

  • Final Strategy Sessions

    Time management, guess elimination, and final NEET-day tactics.
