Refund & Cancellation Policy

We stand behind our courses with a 14-day risk-free trial guarantee

Our Guarantee

✅ 14-Day Risk-Free Trial

We're confident you'll love our courses. If you're not satisfied within 14 days of enrollment, we'll refund your full payment—no questions asked.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 14 days of enrollment
  • You have accessed the course materials
  • You are not satisfied with the course content or teaching methodology
  • The course does not meet your expectations

Refund will NOT be processed if:

  • You request a refund after 14 days of enrollment
  • You have completed more than 50% of the course content
  • You are requesting a refund for reasons unrelated to course quality (e.g., change of mind after significant progress)
  • You have shared your account credentials with others
  • You have violated our Terms and Conditions

Cancellation Policy

Course Cancellation

You can cancel your course enrollment at any time. However, refund eligibility depends on when you cancel:

Cancellation Timeline

  • Within 14 days: Full refund (100%)
  • 15-30 days: 50% refund
  • After 30 days: No refund, but you retain course access until the end of your subscription period

Special Cases

If Abhimanue Academy discontinues a course, all enrolled students will receive a full refund regardless of the enrollment date.

Technical Issues

If you experience persistent technical issues that prevent course access, please contact support. We'll either resolve the issue or provide a full refund.

Duplicate Payments

If you've been charged twice for the same course, we'll immediately refund the duplicate payment.

No Refund for

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get a refund after 14 days?

A: Yes, you can request a 50% refund within 30 days of enrollment. After 30 days, no refund is available.

Q: How long does the refund take?

A: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after approval.

Q: Will I lose access to the course after refund?

A: Yes, your course access will be revoked immediately after the refund is processed.

Q: Can I get a refund if I've completed the course?

A: No, refunds are not available for completed courses.

Q: Is the 3% credit card charge refundable?

A: No, the 3% payment gateway charge is non-refundable.
