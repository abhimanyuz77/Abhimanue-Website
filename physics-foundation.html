24-WEEK CURRICULUM

Physics + Essential Mathematics

01

Phase 1: Mathematical Foundation (Weeks 1-6)

  • Algebra & Functions

    Quadratic equations, polynomials, logarithms, exponentials

  • Trigonometry

    Ratios, identities, equations, inverse functions

  • Coordinate Geometry & Vectors

    2D/3D coordinates, vector operations, dot & cross products

  • Introduction to Calculus

    Limits, differentiation basics, integration fundamentals

02

Phase 2: Mechanics (Weeks 7-12)

  • Kinematics

    Motion in 1D, 2D, projectile motion, relative velocity

  • Laws of Motion

    Newton's laws, friction, circular motion, pseudo forces

  • Work, Energy & Power

    Conservative forces, potential energy, collisions

  • Rotational Motion

    Torque, moment of inertia, angular momentum

03

Phase 3: Heat & Waves (Weeks 13-16)

  • Thermodynamics

    Laws of thermodynamics, heat engines, entropy

  • Kinetic Theory of Gases

    Ideal gas, molecular speeds, degrees of freedom

  • Waves & Oscillations

    SHM, wave equation, superposition, resonance

  • Sound Waves

    Doppler effect, beats, standing waves

04

Phase 4: Electricity & Magnetism (Weeks 17-20)

  • Electrostatics

    Coulomb's law, electric field, potential, capacitors

  • Current Electricity

    Ohm's law, circuits, Kirchhoff's laws, RC circuits

  • Magnetism

    Magnetic field, Lorentz force, Biot-Savart law

  • Electromagnetic Induction

    Faraday's law, Lenz's law, AC circuits

05

Phase 5: Optics & Modern Physics (Weeks 21-24)

  • Ray Optics

    Reflection, refraction, lenses, mirrors, optical instruments

  • Wave Optics

    Interference, diffraction, polarization, Young's experiment

  • Modern Physics Basics

    Photoelectric effect, atomic structure, nuclear physics intro

  • Revision & Problem Solving

    Comprehensive revision, NEET-style problems, exam strategy
