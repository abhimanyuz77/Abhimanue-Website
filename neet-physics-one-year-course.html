CLASS 11 NEET PHYSICS SYLLABUS

01

Physical World & Measurement

  • Units & Measurements

    SI units, significant figures, dimensional analysis, errors.

02

Kinematics

  • Motion in 1D & 2D

    Equations of motion, graphs, projectile motion, circular motion.

03

Laws of Motion

  • Newton's Laws & Friction

    Force analysis, friction, circular motion dynamics, momentum.

04

Work, Energy & Power

  • Energy Conservation

    Work-energy theorem, potential energy, collisions, power.

05

Rotational Motion

  • Centre of Mass & Rotation

    Moment of inertia, torque, angular momentum, rolling motion.

06

Gravitation

  • Gravitational Physics

    Universal law, variation of g, satellites, escape velocity.

07

Properties of Matter

  • Elasticity & Fluids

    Stress-strain, fluid mechanics, viscosity, surface tension.

08

Thermodynamics

  • Heat & Thermodynamics

    Laws of thermodynamics, heat transfer, Carnot engine.

09

Kinetic Theory

  • Kinetic Theory of Gases

    Ideal gas, molecular speeds, degrees of freedom.

10

Oscillations & Waves

  • SHM & Wave Motion

    Simple harmonic motion, wave equation, sound, Doppler effect.
